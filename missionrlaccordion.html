Mission & Vision

Corps Mission & Objectives

Carry out, or procure, research into all factors affecting the safe use of public roads.

Promote and encourage the safe use of public roads by all classes of road users through the circulation of advice, information and knowledge gained from research.

To coneive, develop and implement programmes and courses of action designed to improve road safety, these to include the carrying out of any projects or programmes intended to educate young children or others in the safe use of public roads.
